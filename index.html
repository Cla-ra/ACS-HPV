HPV Vaccination is Cancer Provention

Elimination means never having to think about or worry that you or your children will get a disease.

The HPV vaccine can protect our children's future because it helps prevent six types of cancer later in life.

HPV Vaccination is Cancer Prevention

Prevents most HPV cancers with just 2 HPV Vaccines at age 11 and 12.

HPV vaccination is expected to prevent 90% of HPV cancers.

HPV vaccination is 1 of 3 vaccines all children need at this age.

Globally, cervical cancer is the 4th commonest cancer and leading cause of preventable deaths in women

HPV has been found to the primary cause of cervical cancer.

Cervical cancer is the leading cause of cancer mortality among women in sub-Saharan Africa.

Cancer of the cervix is the third most common cancer in India but accounts for the highest number of cancer deaths.

Using existing tools of vaccines, screening, andtreatment of pre-cancers, we can eliminate HPV cancers, starting with cervical cancer.
